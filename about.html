About Us

E+3 is a corporation based in central Ontario that focuses on creating innovative games for children. In todays world, it is necessary to foster healthy as well as strong relationships amongst families, and friends as technology has advanced to a level that has segregated relationships. E+3 develops games that consist of education, as well as entertainment, plus three various subjects. Through a creative approach to provide a solution to technological advancement, E+3 has the main priority to foster relationships through games.
